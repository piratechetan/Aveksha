    About Us

    Our motto is "From all, for all". We aim to provide the basic healthcare need in the form of drugs and medicines to everyone in need of it but cannot afford or avail it due to lack of proper facilities. We don't have any barriers on who can join and help, and who can avail the facility. It is from all for all!

    Aveksha started with an aim to provide every individual with the basic medications. We primarily focus people who are unfortunate to avail them or unaware of the knowledge.

    Frequently Asked Questioins

    A lot of people ask us various questions like : How we work, What we do with the medicines that we recieve in donation. We try our best to maintain 100% transparency in our working procedure.

    • What we do with the medicines that we recieve?

      We donate these medicines to the poor and needful people and we also take care that no one fakes their financial condition and fools us.

    • How we work?

      We own a store and we use it as an outlet and the people who can't afford expensive medicines can seek for our help and we never fail to help them.

    • Is Aveksha only limited to medicines?

      Basically, Aveksha is made for donation of medicines but our goal is to help all the needful people so we do recieve other donations like clothes, grocery and donate them among the people who need them. We follow our goal "From all, For all".

    • How to Donate?

      We made it very simple for you. Just visit the donate page and fill out the form and we will get back to you. The donation form requires some information and rest all things are handled by us.

    • How to receive medicines?

      You need to visit our outlet store for recieving medicines but if there is an emergency we can deliver the medicines to you under certain terms and condtions. In case anyone faked their emergency situation we can take strict action against them.

    • What you get in return?

      We never let our donators down and we never failed to surprise them. We provide our regular donators with some fixed points and whenever they reach a decided number of points we reward them with various exciting gifts.
